RelieveMe

An app for taxi drivers who are on the go who need to “go”! This app was designed to locate the all Taxi Relief stands around your current location or you can find one in any borough you choose! Once you find a parking space, drivers are able to stay in that space for up to 1-hour to take a nap, run an errand or “go”!
Tech Stack:
Frontend: React
Backend: PostgreSQL + Express
Main Library: react-geocode
Source of APIs: Google Maps JavaScript API
